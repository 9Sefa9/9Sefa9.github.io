About me

Hello!

My name is Sefa. I have studied applied Computer Science (B.Sc.) at university Duisburg-Essen in Germany.
My Bachelor thesis was about developing a framework to extract and classify PDF-Documents through determining Section lengths of Departments in scientific papers taken from IEEE. I absolved my Bachelor study in November 2020 with a grade of 2.8 according to German grading system.

I worked additionally as a teacher in the faculty of distributed systems, which gave me opportunities to improve my technical and communication skills.

In 2015 I led a team of developers, who were responsible for creating high-end games for android mobile devices(Unity3D and C#). In April 2021 I started working as a Full-Stack Developer in the government's department of information and technology Nordrhine-Westphalia (IT.NRW).

Since March 2022 I work as a software engineer and IT-Consultant at CGI.
